Affiliates Module Mod for our keys selling platform.

Module which allows users to earn money by advertising a store by placing reflinks or banners on their sites. Multi-tier commissions scheme (one affiliate may refer another and earn from his referred sales too).

Flexible plans settings per user group and individually. Referred sales tracking. SEO friendly reflinks. Ability to create coupon codes for affiliates.

Money from referred sales added to user wallet with further in-app payouts to PayPal / Skrill / Bank wire accounts.

Adapted for multiple storefronts mod - data is separated.
