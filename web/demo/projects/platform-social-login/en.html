In-site Social Login Mod for our keys selling platform.

A shopping cart engine on which our platform is built came with Janrain engage social add-on by default. Everything was fine until the time when number of sign ins significantly increased and Janrain started charge big money for the service. Then we decided to implement our own in-app module.

We developed our own module which connects to 6 major social sites and its easy to add more. We migrated data from previous Janrain engage module and switched to new one. Now its free and number of connects is limited only by networks restrictions.

At that time we also implemented quick registration with email address only.
