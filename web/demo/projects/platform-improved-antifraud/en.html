Advanced Anti-Fraud Mod for our keys selling platform.

We implemented a lot of special checks and rules to detect and ban fraudsters, or at least not allow them to get a key unless them confirm their identity.

With help of MaxMind our mod additionally checks whole our network if user email, payer ID or payer email exists in block lists, or if user ever opened disputes with charge backs and so on. In case of high risk user will be automatically refunded and wont get his purchase. In case of low risk user need to contact support and confirm his ID.

Mod also allows to set up different rules basing on risk score of gateways and products. Additionally, by one IP and login it can detect all user accounts of same scammer.

And even more useful features.
